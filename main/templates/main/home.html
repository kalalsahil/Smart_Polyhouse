"Polyhouses are not just structures, they are a way of life for modern farmers."

The polyhouse structure allows farmers to cultivate a wide variety of crops with enhanced growth and yield. It provides an ideal growing environment for vegetables such as Cabbage, Bitter Gourd, Capsicum, Radish, Cauliflower, Chili, Coriander, Onion, Spinach, and Tomato, as well as flowers like Carnation, Gerbera, Marigold, Orchid, and Rose. With this technology, farmers can easily manage and regulate the polyhouse without manual intervention..

The polyhouse structure is equipped with advanced IoT-based technology to improve farming and irrigation. Its bio-shed can be regulated based on various factors such as temperature and rainfall. If harsh weather conditions such as heavy rainfall or high temperatures are detected, the shed can be automatically closed or closed manually using a button to protect the crops. Overall, the application of this technology can significantly increase productivity and minimize the risk of crop damage

The polyhouse structure is an advantageous tool for farmers as it enables the cultivation of diverse crop varieties, irrespective of seasonal or adverse conditions. This technology eliminates the need for manual intervention by farmers in the field, allowing for convenient and efficient management of their crops. With the help of a website, farmers can easily regulate and monitor the polyhouse, ensuring optimal growth conditions for their crops
